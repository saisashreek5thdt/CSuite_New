Our Journey

Begining is Half Done.

Kindergarten In Italy

In 2009, Ritzy Group started its first Kindergarten in Bari, Italy as My School ITALY.

Kindergarten In Europe

In 2012, Ritzy Kindergartens expanded to various countries in Europe. Mercedes Benz became our Partner in Germany.

Global Franchising

2015 brought us to Nepal, Bangladesh & India with our European Currirulum and today we have over150 Kindergartens in ten states of India.

C - Suite

2021 sees the launch of Ritzy C-Suite to empower education and foster it for future. This is first of its kind to kindle the Indian education industry.

University

2026 Ritzy will see us entering into the world of Higher Education in many developing nations.
